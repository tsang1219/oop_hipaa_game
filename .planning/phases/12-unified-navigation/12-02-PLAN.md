**Door placement rules:**
- Doors should be placed at logical wall positions (room edges) consistent with the room's tile grid dimensions
- Side walls: `side: 'left'` or `side: 'right'` means x=0 or x=width-1; y is mid-height
- Top/bottom walls: `side: 'top'` or `side: 'bottom'` means y=0 or y=height-1; x is mid-width
- Exact tile coordinates: use the room's width/height to center the door on the appropriate wall
